Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event? −
We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance for weddings and major events, and 3-6 months for corporate events. However, we can accommodate last-minute events depending on availability.
What areas in Tanzania do you serve? +
We serve all major cities across Tanzania including Dar es Salaam, Zanzibar, Arusha, Mwanza, Dodoma, Mbeya, and Tanga. We also plan destination events throughout East Africa.
Do you offer virtual event planning? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive virtual and hybrid event planning services, including technical support, virtual engagement tools, and live streaming production.
What is your payment structure? +
We require a 30% deposit to secure your date, 40% midway through planning, and the remaining 30% two weeks before the event. All payments are detailed in our transparent contracts.
Can you work within my budget? +
Absolutely! We work with budgets of all sizes and will create a custom plan that maximizes your budget while delivering exceptional quality. Use our AI Planner tool to get budget recommendations.
