Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your packages? +

All packages include planning consultations, vendor recommendations, and budget assistance based on our 10 R's methodology. Higher packages include full vendor management, on-site coordination, and custom design services. Exact inclusions vary by package.

How do you implement the 10 R's in your packages? +

Our 10 R's methodology is integrated into every package: Right Planning starts each project, Right Packages are tailored to you, Right Creativity ensures unique designs, Right Team handles execution, Right Communication keeps you informed, Right Experience is curated, Right Time Management ensures deadlines, Right Vendors are selected, Right Reputation is maintained, and Right Results are delivered.

Do you offer month-of coordination only? +

Yes, we offer month-of coordination services starting at $1,500. This includes finalizing details, creating timelines, and on-site coordination on the event day - applying Right Time Management and Right Team principles.

Are there any additional fees? +

Our packages cover planning fees only. Vendor costs, venue fees, decor, catering, and other event expenses are additional and will be included in your overall event budget as part of our Right Planning process.

What is your payment schedule? +

We require a 50% deposit to book your date, 25% at the halfway point, and the remaining 25% two weeks before your event. All payments are outlined in our contract as part of our Right Communication practice.
