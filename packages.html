Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your packages? +

All packages include planning consultations, vendor recommendations, and budget assistance. Higher packages include full vendor management, on-site coordination, and custom design services. Exact inclusions vary by package.

Do you offer month-of coordination only? +

Yes, we offer month-of coordination services starting at $1,500. This includes finalizing details, creating timelines, and on-site coordination on the event day.

Are there any additional fees? +

Our packages cover planning fees only. Vendor costs, venue fees, decor, catering, and other event expenses are additional and will be included in your overall event budget.

What is your payment schedule? +

We require a 50% deposit to book your date, 25% at the halfway point, and the remaining 25% two weeks before your event. All payments are outlined in our contract.
