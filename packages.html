Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our packages

Can I customize my package?

Yes! We specialize in creating custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What payment options are available?

We offer flexible payment plans including installment options. A 30% deposit secures your date, with the balance due 30 days before the event.

Do you handle destination events?

Absolutely! We have experience planning events across Tanzania, from Zanzibar beaches to Serengeti lodges and urban venues in Dar es Salaam.

What's included in "event coordination"?

Our coordination includes vendor management, timeline creation, rehearsal coordination, and day-of management to ensure everything runs smoothly.
